FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

📧 How does the email alias work?

KeepID generates a unique @keepid.me alias for each site directly in the form. Emails are forwarded to your real inbox. Your real address is never exposed.

🔐 Can KeepID read my passwords?

No. Your data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your device. Even we can't read it — that's Zero-Knowledge.

🔥 What happens when I "Burn" an alias?

The alias is disabled immediately — spam stops instantly. You have 30 days to recover it from the extension's trash. After that, it's permanently deleted.

📥 How do I import my existing passwords?

Compatible with 1Password (CSV), Bitwarden (JSON), Apple Keychain (CSV), Chrome, Safari and Firefox. Settings → Import my existing accounts.

🔑 I lost my master password

Use your emergency recovery kit. Without that code, access is permanently lost — write it on paper.
